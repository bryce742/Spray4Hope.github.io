Our Mission Statement

"To say the opioid crisis is an epidemic is an understatement. Over 100,000 people die every year due to overdose; that is why our goal is to improve Narcan accessibility across the United states. Overdose is often a catalyst for the motivation to get clean, but getting clean requires surviving rock bottom."
